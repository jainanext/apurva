Vision for JAINA and the Southwest Region:
JAINA is the organization that unifies all Jain Centers and their members across North America. As our community
grows, I want to see JAINA bring a strong common identity to all Jains in North America, while still allowing the
diversity of the Jain religion to continue to flourish at the local level. Being born and brought up in the United
States, I believe I bring a unique experience that allows me to understand the needs of all generations of Jains in
North America. Below are the key benefits that I will bring to JAINA, the JAINA EC and the Southwest region if
elected as Southwest Regional VP.
Working with the JAINA Core Team to promoting existing JAINA activities (like YJA, YJP, Education, etc) while
encouraging new activities that help support our growing communities (e.g. senior housing, JAINA Students
Guidance Committee, etc).
Using my leadership skills as a business CEO to help nurture the next generation to be active in their local
centers and at the JAINA level with Jain programs, activities and leadership opportunities geared to engage
the younger generations.
Growing the bond between Jain centers in the Southwest region, which are growing very fast due to the
changing demographics of the United States.
Using my position in JAINA to help support the needs of the growing Southwest region in areas like temple
construction, seva activities, community support, youth and senior activities, etc.
